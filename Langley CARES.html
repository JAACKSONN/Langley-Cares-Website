Our Goal

As the COVID-19 Pandemic continues to grow, homeless shelters, schools with less resources, hospitals, and nursing homes are in need of tablets/iPads/technology in order to facilitate communication between folks in isolation. We are a Langley High School group of concerned students trying to reach out to everyone in our community to try and help those in need during this pandemic. Since visitors are not typically allowed in these places, it is crucial that these individuals get the equipment they need.

Help Fund

If you want to do your part an help those in need, you can donate by clicking one of the buttons below: PayPal or Venmo, whichever you prefer. To put your money into perspective, a $50 donation will provide children or seniors in need of a tablet with an Amazon Fire 7 that can be used for a variety of tasks, from talking to their families (which they otherwise may not have been able to do) to learning new things during this time of isolation. Individuals, like COVID patients, are not always given the resources they need; so, any donation will help greatly.

Donating Old Technology

If you own any form of working technology that you feel could be useful to someone, please, drop off your tablets, extra phones, and old laptops into one of the boxes at the addresses below. Please drop off between the hours of 8 AM and 10 PM. Additionally, please knock or ring the door bell when dropping off. There will be no contact, but we do not want to leave items outside for too long. Please place the donated technology in the bin outside of the house. Electronics do not need to be donated with a charger. All passwords should be removed from devices before donating. McLean address: 8216 Hunting Hill Lane, Mclean, VA 22102. Great Falls address: 10907 Great Point Court, Great Falls, VA 22066.
